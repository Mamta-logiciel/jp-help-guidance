Configuration Trade Types

This page displays the trades your company operates in. The functionalities that can be performed using Trade Types are as explained below:

  • Go to Settings, click on Trade Types from the left panel.
  • You can add more trade types that your company operates in from this page. Just choose and select the radio buttons next to the trades you want to add.
  • Hit Save
